‚õî Rejection Therapy


Many of life's opportunities can only be accessed by making big asks.

In a way‚Äî your potential hangs on your ability to step outside your comfort zone and ask for what you want.

& the truth is, many never ask for what they want out of fear of rejection.

Rejection Therapy is the prescription for that fear.


How Rejection Therapy Works

Rejection Therapy is a challenge to get rejected by someone, every day.

You need to do outlandish things, like ask a stranger for a ride to Tijuana, Mexico.

When they say no, you've won for the day.

The point of the game is to overcome fear of rejection by exposing yourself to it repeatedly.

Eventually we start to detach from our "egos" & rejection no longer damages our self-esteem.

Developing this mental toughness grants us access to step outside our comfort zone and seek opportunities that others won't.

Things like asking a stranger on a date, requesting a promotion, or shipping your creative side hustle to the world become first nature when that fear of rejection is eliminated. 


Here's some questions you can try this week on strangers:

Could I have a bite of your sandwich?

Can you drive me to Tijuana, Mexico?

Could you give me a back scratch?

Could I wear your shoes?


If anyone wants to share whacky rejection therapy questions I'll send back a video of me asking them to strangers.

üåê Network w/ Lunchclub


How do you network while working from home? Answer:  Lunchclub.

Discovered this beauty a few weeks ago thanks to an invite from my friend Mike Setuain.

Lunchclub's free & gives you "smart introductions to relevant people."

It makes "curated 1:1 professional connections."


How does it work?

When you join they ask a series of questions about your interests, goals, & ambitions. 

Their algorithm uses this to match you with similar people to make the best possible connection.

Then you & your match video-call for 45 minutes about how you can help each other with your goals, projects, & interests.

It's simple & beautiful.

Only catch, you can't join right away. 

They're largely invite only & there's a waiting list if you don't know someone that's already a member.
