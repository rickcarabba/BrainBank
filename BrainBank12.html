🔌 Tech that Improves Focus


We now have shorter attention spans than goldfish. ( Microsoft Insights, Pg. 6)

And it makes sense. Many business model's are now built to monetize our attention. 


How do they monetize our attention? 

More time on your screen equals more behavioral data collected.

More behavioral data equals more refined Ad segments. 

More refined Ad segments equals very targeted ad campaigns (with a higher probability that you will buy something from an advertiser.) 

Very targeted ad campaigns equals more money advertisers will pay to reach you. 

For this reason, many companies design their products to optimize for time spent on the platform. 

If you feel an urge to use an app, scroll your feed, or pick up your phone—it was probably designed that way (for more on this read  Hooked: How to build habit forming products).

A lot of products add value to our lives but i t's important to be aware of the things that are influencing how you spend your time.  


How can we rebuild our attention spans?

Here's my 5 part stack for rebuilding attention span.

Browser:

1. RescueTime

  • Desktop extension that measures your screen time, where it's spent, and how you can improve. 

2. Cold Turkey Software

  • Zucks tugging at your attention? Tiktok too entertaining? 
  • Use Cold Turkey so you don't need to expend willpower. It blocks apps, sites, email etc. with no exceptions. You set the time period. 

Mobile: 

3. Moment App

  • See real time data about where you spend your time. Like Apple's Screen Time but better (IMO).

4. Flora App (recent favorite)

  • Grow trees, block apps, feel good.
  • Set a focus time, if you leave the app for distractions before your time runs out then your tree dies.
  • They really plant the trees you grow. 

5. Timed Lockboxes

  • Lock phone, addictions, etc. in the box for a set period of time (up to 10 days). 
  • Lockbox can't open until timer runs out, no override. 
If this topic interests you, I also suggest  Deep Work by Cal Newport. 


Your attention & time are finite currencies. 

Be frugal with where you spend them.

🍼 Increasing Children's IQ

For anyone gettin busy, here's 3 conclusions from "How to Make a Young Child Smarter" by the Association of Psychological Science. 


1.  1000mg supplements of fatty acids (DHA Omega 3s) per day to pregnant women shows 3.5 point increase in their child's IQ (Pg. 27) 

  • DHAs provide building blocks for nerve cell development to create new synapses in the brain. 

2.  Reading to a child ages 4 and younger raises IQ by over 6 points, especially if child is asked to elaborate (Pg. 32)

  • Make a household rule that you can't ask 1 word questions. Forces elaboration. 

3. Attending a preschool raises a young child's IQ by as much as 7 points, especially if there's a specific language development component (Pg. 33)
